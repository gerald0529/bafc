Business alliance financial circle,abbreviated as BAFC,initiated by the BAFC foundation of the Australian, jointly organized by commercial alliances around the world to solve the problems in current international financial and trade, and to build a new generation of international business alliance ecosphere, so as to promote the rapid development of international trade and economic globalization.

Application scene

  • Financial payment

    The core of finance is the establishment and transmission of credit. Linkage chain makes full use of the characteristics of unfalsifiable, safe, transparent, decentralized or multi-centralized block chain in international financial payment, and builds a flat global payment system to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

  • Cross-border payment

    Linkage chain applies block chain technology to cross-border payment, and builds payment gateway by adding traditional financial institutions, foreign exchange market makers, liquidity providers and so on. Through the payment gateway, the digital asset flow on the block chain can be linked with the real legal tender, and the legal tender and bafc can be converted to facilitate the subsequent payment and transfer. The traditional market maker, outward remittance bank, inward remittance bank and other institutions can be connected, and the intermediate transaction link can be abandoned to achieve point-to-point rapid and low-cost payment.

  • Liquidation and settlement

    The process of clearing and settling transactions is also the process of recording accounts separately between the two parties. The linked chain block chain system enables the two or more parties to share a set of credible and mutually recognized accounts. All the clearing and settling records are traceable in the chain, which is safe, transparent, unauthorized and traceable, greatly improving the accuracy and efficiency of reconciliation. By carrying intelligent contracts, automatic transaction clearing and settlement can be achieved, which greatly reduces the cost of reconciliation personnel and error rate.

  • Trace to the source of goods

    The alliance chain uses block chain technology to build a centralized cloud platform through intelligent contracts. Based on the contract to complete the verification of traceability anti-counterfeiting codes and the inquiry of logistics codes, the consulting chain introduces the latest Internet of Things RFID technology and AI artificial intelligence while making use of the block chain technology. Starting from the source of the commodity, the consulting chain begins block recording at the beginning of the design of the commodity, and then proceeds to the purchase, production, assembly, molding and packing of raw materials. The whole production process and sales process, such as delivery, are all recorded on the chain, which fundamentally guarantees the real safety of the goods and achieves the real anti-counterfeiting traceability.

  • Commodity circulation

    By using the block chain technology and making full use of the features of the block chain technology, such as decentralization, traceability and unusurpable modification, the whole process of commodity circulation is linked up and all links are recorded and solved in the block chain, thus reducing the unnecessary links in the middle, greatly improving the efficiency of commodity circulation, and at the same time greatly reducing the efficiency of commodity circulation. The cost of commodities plays a significant role in promoting the circulation of international commodities.
